# Global Aviation Accident Analysis

**From Chaos to Control: How Data Tells the Story of Aviation Safety**

From chaos to control, aviation safety has evolved through decades of lessons written in data. This project, Global Aviation Accident Analysis, explores historical aviation accident records to uncover patterns behind fatal accidents, operator performance, geographic trends, and longâ€‘term safety improvements.

Using data analytics and visualization, the project transforms raw accident records into insights that help answer one key question:

How has global aviation safety changed over time, and what factors have contributed to improvements or risks?


Objectives

-The main goals of this analysis were to:

-Examine historical trends in aviation accidents and fatalities

-Identify periods of high risk and major improvements in safety

-Analyze accident distribution across regions and operators

-Understand the relationship between accident frequency and fatal outcomes

-Communicate findings clearly through an interactive Power BI dashboard
